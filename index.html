ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm an aerospace engineer specialized in space propulsion, with a Master's degree in Space Engineering from the University of Pisa. I focus on electric propulsion systems, particularly iodine-fed Hall thrusters, and work on numerical simulations to study thermal dissociation, discharge stability, and thruster–power system interactions using MATLAB-based models.

My goal is to contribute to advanced propulsion technologies that expand the possibilities of space exploration.

Alongside engineering, I have a strong interest in art and illustration. I enjoy exploring the intersection of science and visual thinking, and this creative perspective supports a more flexible and thoughtful approach to problem-solving.
